Chrysteraki

  • Name
  • Characteristics

Chrysteraki is the stellar body in the K2-776 system, which is located in the Makriastron galaxy. It is most notable for having two habitable planets, both of which harbor native lifeforms. It has 9 total planets, four of which are terrestrial, and the other five being gas or ice giants.

Name

The name Chrysteraki comes from the Greek words "χρυσ-" and "αστεράκι", meaning "little golden star". This is due to the fact that it is much smaller than our sun, and much more yellow. The star may also be referred to as K2-776.
