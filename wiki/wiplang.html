This conlang was inspired by r/conlangscirclejerk. As such, it is meant to be as bad as possible, intentionally of course. This is achieved with a fairly obnoxious phonology, and terrible grammar.

Phonotactics

WIPLang has a CV(C) consonant structure. /w/ and /j/ are the only consonants that are not permitted in C2. Two ejectives are not permitted next to one another(i.e. no /ħɛqʼtʼɑ/). Plosives in C2 position may be unreleased. Affricates behave like plosives. C2 nasals assimilate to C1 plosives and fricatives. Palatal allophones are found before front vowels, velar before back. Vowel allophones are triggered by open syllables and uvular and pharyngeal consonants.

Orthography

WIPLang has a mostly phonetic romanization. Due to its phonology, WIPLang cannot use ASCII-only characters. As such, diacritics are used on both vowel and consonant letters. The following tables display the romanization of every phoneme.
