This is a complete phonological history of Liró. Several stages occurred, such as the split from Eastern Daimarian into Riparian, Central, and Peninsular, but those are not mentioned; Only approximate dates are mentioned. It should also be noted that the dates given are the approximate points at which the changes are thought to have completed by, not when they started.
