Chrysouli is the central body of the Chrysouli system.

Name

Chrysouli comes from the Greek words χρυσ-, "golden", and -ούλι, a diminutive suffix, vaguely meaning "little golden". This name arises from the fact that the star is a fair amount smaller than our sun, as well as being much more yellow.
