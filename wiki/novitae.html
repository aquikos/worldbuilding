Novitae is the fourth planet from the star Chrysouli, and one of two planets within the system to harbor life.

Name

Novitae comes from the Latin words novī, "new", and vītae, "life", meaning "new life". Novitae may also be referred to as Chrysouli D, in reference to its parent star, and position from said star.
