Novitae

Novitae is the fourth planet from the star Chrysteraki, and the only planet in the system other than Neosaia to harbor life.

Name

The name Novitae comes from the Latin words "novī" and "vītae", meaning "new life". This is because Novitae was the first non-Earth planet discovered to harbor life. It may also be referred to as Chrysteraki D, or K2-776 D, referring to its parent star, and position from said star.
