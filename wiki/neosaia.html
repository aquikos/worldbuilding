Neosaia is the third planet from the star Chrysouli, and one of two planets within the system to harbor life.

Name

Neosaia comes from the Greek words νέος-, "new", and -αἶα, "Earth", meaning "new earth". Neosaia is also referred to as Chrysouli C, indicating its parent star, and position from said star.
