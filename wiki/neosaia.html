Neosaia

  • Name
  • Characteristics

Neosaia is the third planet from the star Chrysteraki. It and the planet Novitae harbor life, with Neosaia harboring humans as well.

Name

The name comes from the Greek words "νέος-" and "-αἶα", meaning "new Earth". The planet is also referred to as Chrysteraki C, and, more rarely, K2-776C, both denoting the parent star, and the relative position from said star.
